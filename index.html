About Mike

Mike Binder is a comedian, writer, and director. He has written and directed many feature films including The Upside of Anger and Reign Over Me, and wrote and directed the Showtime five-part docuseries The Comedy Store (Available on Amazon Prime). He's also the editor in chief of Standupworld and Standupworld.substack.com (Buttons to both above.)
