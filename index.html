Do You Want to Know Who the Next Nate Bargatze Is?

Ask Nate Bargatze.

If you’re not already watching Aaron Weber, pause whatever you’re doing — including reading this — and hit play on his new special, Signature Dish, streaming now on YouTube. It's the first full-length special under the Nateland banner, and Aaron’s the first guy through the gate. That tells you everything you need to know.

I met him one night at Zanies in Nashville — totally unplanned — and he blew me away. He’s not flashy, not gimmicky. He just writes and performs like someone who’s been doing it for 20 years. Smart, steady, honest comedy. A true pro.

So who is Aaron Weber? He’s the third chair on Nate's podcast, Nateland, and the guy who looks like he might also run a bait shop on weekends. But his jokes? Tight. Timed. Terrific.

Signature Dish is everything great about clean comedy — and harder to do than most people think. It’s a real calling card. And it proves Aaron's got the stuff to headline theaters and build a huge fanbase.
